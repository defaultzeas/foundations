Peanut butter chocolate cereal

Description

The long time champion of breakfast foods but with some improvements.

Cereal is traditionally quite simple, involving a cereal of your choice and some milk. However, this version has added walnuts and peanut butter, which is all mixed and heated to delicious perfection.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chocolate muesli
  • ½ cup crunchy chocolate cereal
  • ½ handful walnuts
  • 2 teaspoons peanut butter
  • 1 ½ cups milk (can be non dairy milk)

Directions

  1. Step

    Fill half a cereal bowl with a mix of the crunchy chocolate cereal and chocolate muesli.

  2. Step

    Add walnuts and peanut butter and fill the rest of the bowl with the mixture from step 1.

  3. Step

    Add the milk of your choice and heat for 45 seconds in a microwave.
